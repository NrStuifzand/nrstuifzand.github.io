Why You Should Rescue a Dog or Puppy

If you have decided to invite a furry friend into your life, congratulations! If you are thinking about rescuing a dog instead of buying one, you should! There are many benefits when adopting or rescuing a dog instead of buying one; this doesn’t mean that buying one is bad mind you.

 

There a ton of dogs in shelters looking for homes

An estimated of 26 dogs are in each shelter waiting for a forever home to go to. Shelters also have varying types, ages, and sizes of dogs, so with time, you can find the dog that is perfect for you. Plus, you then know exactly what kind of dog they are and how big they are.

 

You will feel like a better person

You will feel like a better person because your canine friend will always be there for you and give you unconditional love no matter what. You'll also feel better because you rescued them from a shelter and gave them a safe and loving home.

 

You will be providing them with a home

By adopting or rescuing a dog, you are providing them with a safe home where they are comfortable and happy.
